Andrano (Salentino: 'Ndranu) is a town and comune in the province of Lecce in the Apulia region of south-east Italy.

Main sights[edit]

  • Church of St. Andrew the Apostle
  • Church of St. Dominic
  • Spinola-Caracciolo castle
