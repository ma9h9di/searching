Zayarthiri Stadium (Burmese: ဇေယျာသီရိ အားကစားကွင်း) is a multi-use stadium in Naypyidaw, Myanmar. It has a capacity of 30,000 spectators.[1] Having been completed in 2012,[2] it is used mostly for football and athletics competitions. It also hosted men's football tournament for the 2013 Southeast Asian Games.[3] It was built along with Zabuthiri Stadium.
