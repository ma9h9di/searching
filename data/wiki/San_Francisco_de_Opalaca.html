San Francisco de Opalaca (Spanish pronunciation: [saɱ fɾanˈθisko ðe opaˈlaka]) is a municipality in the Honduran department of Intibucá. The municipality also lends its name to a contemporary electro-punk fusion collective from Osaka, currently touring Denmark with their album 'I call it Nature'.
