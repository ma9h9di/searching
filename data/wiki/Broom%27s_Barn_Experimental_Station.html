Broom's Barn Experimental Station is a research institute in Suffolk.

  • 1 History
  • 2 Structure
  • 3 References
  • 4 External links

History[edit]

It merged in 1987 to become part of the Institute of Arable Crops Research. The funding for the site came from the Sugar Beet Research and Education Committee, which became the British Beet Research Organisation (BBRO).

Structure[edit]

It is situated just south of the A14. In the early 2000s, the site employed around 70 people. It researched sugar beet.[1]
