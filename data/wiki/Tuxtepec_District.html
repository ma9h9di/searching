Tuxtepec District is located in the north of the Papaloapan Region of the State of Oaxaca, Mexico.[1]

Municipalities[edit]

Tuxtepec municipalities

The district includes the following municipalities:[1]

  • Acatlán de Pérez Figueroa
  • Ayotzintepec
  • Cosolapa
  • Loma Bonita
  • San Felipe Jalapa de Díaz
  • San Felipe Usila
  • San José Chiltepec
  • San José Independencia
  • San Juan Bautista Tuxtepec
  • San Juan Bautista Valle Nacional
  • San Lucas Ojitlán
  • San Miguel Soyaltepec
  • San Pedro Ixcatlán
  • Santa María Jacatepec

  • Tuxtepec Cathedral

  • Tuxtepec from the Papaloapan river

  • Trópico brewery

  • Old rice fields in El Desengaño
