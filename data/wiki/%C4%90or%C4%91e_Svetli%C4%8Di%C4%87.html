Đorđe Svetličić (Serbian Cyrillic: Ђорђе Светличић; born 5 January 1974) is a retired Serbian international footballer who played as a defender. He has 2 son’s: Igor(15) Andrej(12)

Club career[edit]

Svetličić spent six seasons with Partizan, before moving to Germinal Beerschot in 1999. He also played for Cercle Brugge and Gent, before returning to Germinal Beerschot.

International career[edit]

Svetličić made his international debut for FR Yugoslavia in a friendly against Russia on 20 August 1997. He played his second and last match for the team in another friendly against Argentina on 25 February 1998.
