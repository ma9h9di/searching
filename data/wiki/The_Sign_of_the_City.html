The Sign of the City (Portuguese: O Signo da Cidade) is a 2007 Brazilian drama film directed by Carlos Alberto Riccelli and written by Bruna Lombardi, who also plays the main character.

Plot[edit]

Gil (Malvino Salvador) is married, but is lonely. Lydia (Denise Fraga) likes to take chances. Josialdo (Sidney Santiago) was born to be a woman. Monica (Graziella Moretto) is a Gold Digger and just wants to get along. Everybody listens to the radio night program of the astrologer Teca (Bruna Lombardi), which deals with the aspirations of its listeners and her own problems.[1]
