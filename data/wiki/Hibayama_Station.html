Hibayama Station (比婆山駅, Hibayama-eki) is a JR West Geibi station located in Ōya, Saijō-chō, Shōbara, Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan.

History[edit]

  • 1987-04-01: Japan National Railways is privatized, and Hibayama Station becomes a JR West station

Station building, platform and environs[edit]

Hibayama Station features one ground-level platform. The Saijō River is located about 100m behind the station.

Highway access[edit]

  • Japan National Route 183
  • Hiroshima Prefectural Route 254 (Hibayama Kōen Route)

Connecting lines[edit]

All lines are JR West lines.
