Murchison (biogeographic region)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
The IBRA regions, with Murchison in red

Murchison is an Interim Biogeographic Regionalisation for Australia (IBRA) region in Western Australia[1][2] and part of the Western Australian Mulga shrublands ecoregion.[3]
