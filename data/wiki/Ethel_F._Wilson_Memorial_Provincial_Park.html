Ethel F. Wilson Memorial Provincial Park is a provincial park in central British Columbia, Canada. The park is situated on northern tip of Pinkut lake. Activities in the park include rainbow trout fishing, and boating. The park has 5 campsites that are allocated on a first-come first-save basis. The area of the park is 33 hectares.
