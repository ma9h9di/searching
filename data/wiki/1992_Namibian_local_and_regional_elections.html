Local Authority Council and Regional Council Elections in Namibia were held from 30 November to 3 December 1992.

Local Authority Council Results[edit]

e • d Summary of the 30 November-3 December Namibia Local election results[1]
Party Votes (%) % Seats
South West Africa People's Organization 73,736 57.17 184
Democratic Turnhalle Alliance 42,278 32.78 116
United Democratic Front 7,473 5.79 18
South West African National Union (SWANU) 1,895 1.47 16
Worker's Revolutionary Party (WRP) 115 0.09 0
Local Associations 1,561 1.18 1
Invalid votes 1,879
Total 128,973 100 320
