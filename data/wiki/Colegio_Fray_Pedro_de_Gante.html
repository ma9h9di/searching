Colegio Fray Pedro de Gante is a Roman Catholic school in Nogales, Sonora. It was founded in 1946 by Father Ignacio de la Torre (commonly referred to as Padre Nacho). From 2010-2012, it was an associate member of the Arizona Interscholastic Association for the purpose of scrimmaging with Arizona high schools in football. All matches were played in Arizona.

School levels[edit]

The school is divided into four levels:

  • Preescolar/Jardín de Niños (equivalent to kindergarten)
  • Primaria (equivalent to US grades 1-6)
  • Secundaria (equivalent to US grades 7-9)
  • Preparatoria (equivalent to US grades 10-12)
