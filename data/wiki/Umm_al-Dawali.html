Umm al-Dawali (Arabic: أم الدوالي‎) is a village in northern Syria located northwest of Homs in the Homs Governorate. According to the Syria Central Bureau of Statistics, Umm al-Dawali had a population of 1,558 in the 2004 census.[1] Its inhabitants are predominantly Alawites.[2]
