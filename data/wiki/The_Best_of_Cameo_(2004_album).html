The Best of Cameo is a compilation album released by the funk/R&B group Cameo in 2004. It is not to be confused with the 1993 release The Best of Cameo. However, it is a repackaging of the 1998 issue of Best of Cameo, but with "The" added to the album's title, even using the same cover art. A more complete, career-spanning compilation, Gold, was released in 2005.[1] < [2] [3] [4]
