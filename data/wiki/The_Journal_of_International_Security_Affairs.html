The Journal of International Security Affairs

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Journal of International Security Affairs is an American electronic journal on international relations and U.S. foreign and defense policy published twice annually by the Jewish Institute for National Security Affairs.[1] It was established in 2001 and its editor-in-chief is Ilan Berman (American Foreign Policy Council).[1]

The headquarters of the Journal of International Security Affairs is in Washington, D.C.[1] From 2016 the magazine became a free publication.[2]
