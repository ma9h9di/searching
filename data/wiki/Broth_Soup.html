Broth Soup (Persian: آش نذری‎) is a 2000 Iranian film written and directed by Hossein Shahabi (Persian: حسین شهابی) [1][2][3][4]

Starring[edit]

  • Ali Piroozi
  • Rahman Ghorbani
  • Hashem Saeedi
  • Nader Golrokh
  • Sadegh Sharifian
  • Ahmad Abbasi

Cast[edit]

  • producer: Kobra Ghasemi
  • Production manager: Mnsour Sharifian

  • Sound Recorder: Ali Kohzad
  • Costume Designer: Fariba Mortazavi
  • Make Up: Sanaz Shahin
  • Music: Hossein Shahabi
  • Production: Kish TV
