Di Balik 98 (Behind 98, formerly Di Balik Pintu Istana, Behind The Palace Door) is an Indonesian drama film directed by Lukman Sardi, starring Chelsea Islan and Boy William. It was released on January 15, 2015.[1]The film is themed around the fall of Suharto's regime in 1998.[2]
