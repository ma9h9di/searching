Cuzznz is the collaborative studio album by the American West coast hip hop recording artists Daz Dillinger and Snoop Dogg. It was released on January 15, 2016, by Felder Entertainment Inc.[2] Cuzznz has guest appearances from Dâm-funk, Kurupt and Shon'Lawon.

Singles[edit]

The track "Sho You Right" was released on SoundCloud on October 26, 2015.[3] The song was produced by Dâm-funk.[4]

"Best Friend" was leaked via SoundCloud on December 3, 2015,[5] and released along with the pre-order of the album on same day. It was officially released as the album's first single. The song was produced by kjconteh.[6]

On January 14, 2016, "N My Life Tyme" was released.[7] The song was produced by Dâm-funk.
