Chit San Maung (born 25 January 1988) is a footballer from Burma, and plays as a defender for Ayeyawady United. He is not to be confused with the guitarist of the same name who was a member of Saw Bwe Hmu's band.

On 31 May 2010 in an 2010 AFC President's Cup game against FC HTTU Chit was sent off in the 29 minute in an eventual 0-0 draw where HTTU's Arslanmyrat Amanow was also sent off.[3]

In the 2015 AFC Cup group stage Chit conceded a first half penalty and was sent off in the second half against Lao Toyota F.C., although Ayeyawady eventually won the match 4-3.[4]

Retired in December 2015.
