Custos Rotulorum of County Londonderry and Down

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Custos Rotulorum of Londonderry and Down was the highest civil officer in counties Londonderry and Down.

Incumbents[edit]

This list is incomplete; you can help by expanding it.

Londonderry[edit]

  • 1663–1665 John Clotworthy, 1st Viscount Massereene [1]
  • 1666–?1695 John Skeffington, 2nd Viscount Massereene (attainted 1689, reappointed 1693, died 1695) [2]

Down[edit]

  • 1660–1663 Hugh Montgomery, 1st Earl of Mount Alexander
  • 1663–? William Montgomery (died 1706) [3]
  • 1678–?1693 Michael Hill (died 1693) [4] (also Custos Rotulorum of Antrim 1678-?)
  • ?1693–?1699 Michael Hill (died 1699)[5]
  • 1729–1742 Trevor Hill, 1st Viscount Hillsborough (died 1742) [6]
  • 1742–1793 Wills Hill, 1st Marquess of Downshire [7]
  • 1793–?1801 Arthur Hill, 2nd Marquess of Downshire (died 1801)

Londonderry and Down[edit]

  • 1803–?1821 Robert Stewart, 1st Marquess of Londonderry [8]
  • 1821–1822 Robert Stewart, Viscount Castlereagh [9]
  • 1822–?1854 Charles Vane, 3rd Marquess of Londonderry (died 1854) [9]

For later custodes rotulorum, see Lord Lieutenant of Londonderry and Lord Lieutenant of Down
