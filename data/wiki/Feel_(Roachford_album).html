Feel is the fourth album by British band Roachford, released in 1997. The album includes three singles which reached the UK Singles Chart: "The Way I Feel" (UK #20), "How Could I? (Insecurity)" (UK #34), and "Naked Without You" (UK #53).[2] "Naked Without You" was later recorded by singer Taylor Dayne for her 1998 album of the same name, along with another Andrew Roachford-written song included on this album, "Don't Make Me Love You".
