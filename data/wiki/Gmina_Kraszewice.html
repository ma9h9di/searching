Gmina Kraszewice is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Ostrzeszów County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Kraszewice, which lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) north-east of Ostrzeszów and 133 km (83 mi) south-east of the regional capital Poznań.

The gmina covers an area of 75.11 square kilometres (29.0 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 3,621.

Villages[edit]

Gmina Kraszewice contains the villages and settlements of Głuszyna, Jaźwiny, Jelenie, Kraszewice, Kuźnica Grabowska, Mączniki, Racławice and Renta.

Neighbouring gminas[edit]

Gmina Kraszewice is bordered by the gminas of Brzeziny, Czajków, Grabów nad Prosną and Sieroszewice.
