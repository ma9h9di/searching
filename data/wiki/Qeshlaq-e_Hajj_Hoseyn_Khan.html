Qeshlaq-e Hajj Hoseyn Khan (Persian: قشلاق حاج حسين خان‎, also Romanized as Qeshlāq-e Ḩājj Ḩoseyn Khān) is a village in Qeshlaq-e Sharqi Rural District, Qeshlaq Dasht District, Bileh Savar County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 139, in 31 families.[1]
