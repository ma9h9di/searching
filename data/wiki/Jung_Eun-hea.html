Jung Eun-hea (born 27 September 1989) is a South Korean sport shooter.

Jung graduated from Kangwon Sports High School [ko]. She won the silver medal in women's 10 metre air rifle at the 2018 Asian Games.[1] She participated at the 2018 ISSF World Shooting Championships.[2]
