Leirvíkar Ítróttarfelag, commonly known as LÍF or LÍF Leirvík, was a Faroese football club based in Leirvík. The club was founded in 1928, but they merged with GÍ Gøta 75 years later in 2008 to form Víkingur.

From 1982 to 1989 and in 1993 it played in the county's highest league. In 1986 Leirvík lost the Faroe Islands Cup final.

Honours[edit]

  • 1. deild: 2 (1981, 1992)
  • 2. deild: 1 (1980)
