Rodnover Confederation is a confederation of Polish Slavic Native Faith religious organizations, founded on 23 August 2015 during the 3rd All-Poland Rodnover Congress in Łysogóry.

Since 2016 the Rodnover Confederation has organizer the All-Poland Rodnover Congress and publisher the Rodnover Calendar.[1] In May 2016, after a break of 500 years, on behalf of the Rodnover Confederation, the first celebration of the Stado festival was organized. It was the largest ceremony of this kind in the modern history of the Polish Rodnover movement.[2]
