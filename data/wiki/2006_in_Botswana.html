Incumbents[edit]

  • President: Festus Mogae
  • Vice President: Ian Khama

Events[edit]

December[edit]

  • December 13 - The High Court of Botswana rules that thousands of Bushmen should not have been evicted from their ancestral home which is now the Central Botswana Game Reserve.[1]
