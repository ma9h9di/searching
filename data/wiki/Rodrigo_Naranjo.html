Rodrigo David Naranjo López (born 30 August 1979) is a Chilean footballer who currently plays for Chilean Primera División club Deportes Iquique as goalkeeper.

Honours[edit]

Club[edit]

Santiago Wanderers
  • Primera División de Chile (1): 2001
Deportes Iquique
  • Primera B (1): 2010
  • Copa Chile (2): 2010, 2012–13
