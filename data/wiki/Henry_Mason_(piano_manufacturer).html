Henry Mason (piano manufacturer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Henry Mason (1831–1890) was one of the co-founders of the American piano manufacturer Mason and Hamlin.[1]

He was the son of American church music composer Lowell Mason, and the brother of composer William Mason.
