Kenichi Nozawa (野澤 健一, Nozawa Kenichi, born January 27, 1984) is a former Japanese football player.[1]

Playing career[edit]

Kenichi Nozawa played for JEF Reserves, Sagawa Printing and AC Nagano Parceiro from 2006 to 2014.
