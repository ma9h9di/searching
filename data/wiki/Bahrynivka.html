Bahrynivka

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Bahrynivka (Ukrainian: Багринівка; Romanian: Bahrinești) is a commune (selsoviet) in Hlyboka Raion, Chernivtsi Oblast, Ukraine. It is composed of a single village, Bahrynivka.
