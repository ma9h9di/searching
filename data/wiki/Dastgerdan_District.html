Dastgerdan District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Not to be confused with Dastgerdan Rural District.

Dastgerdan District (Persian: بخش دستگردان‎) is a district (bakhsh) in Tabas County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 11,659, in 3,246 families.[1] The District has one city: Eshqabad. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Dastgerdan Rural District and Kuh Yakhab Rural District.
