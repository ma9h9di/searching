Jacob Kelly "Jake" Bass (born February 22, 1991) is a Canadian model, influencer, and former gay adult film star. His career in the adult film industry started at the beginning of 2011 and ended mid-2015.

Career[edit]

In March 2011, Squirtz and Videoboys, both based in Montreal, released Bass’s first scenes.[2][3]

Bass was featured in the mini-series Project GoGo Boy, for which in 2013, he won alongside the coprotagonist Max Ryder the Grabby Awards "Best Newcomers" for his performance.

In 2014, while Bass was on the book tour of A Thing of Beauty, Benjamin Cook, an English writer, journalist and filmmaker, filmed Bass and Max Ryder for his online documentary series, Tofu,[4] which was released in 2015.

In 2016, Wilder Green released his debut music video Naked[5] which featured Bass. Naked was directed by Trevor Cox and produced by PotsNPans Films.

Bass has appeared on the cover of magazines such as Gay Times, Männer Magazine, lovesexo, and Fantastics Magazine[6], as well as appeared in spreads for ADON, Coitus, and Kuhl.

Personal life[edit]

Bass was born and raised in Montreal, Quebec. His father died when he was sixteen years old.[7]

Regarding his sexual orientation, Bass has claimed that he doesn’t like labels and has had relationships with both men and women.[8]
