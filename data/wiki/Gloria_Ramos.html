Gloria Ramos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Gloria Deniz Ramos Prudencio is a Peruvian politician and a Congresswoman representing Pasco for the 2006-2011 term. Ramos belongs to the Union for Peru party.
