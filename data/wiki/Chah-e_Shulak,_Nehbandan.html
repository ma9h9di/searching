Chah-e Shulak (Persian: چاه شولك‎, also Romanized as Chāh-e Shūlaḵ) is a village in Bandan Rural District, in the Central District of Nehbandan County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 134, in 27 families.[1]
