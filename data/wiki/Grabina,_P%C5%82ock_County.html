Grabina [ɡraˈbina] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łąck, within Płock County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Łąck, 9 km (6 mi) south of Płock, and 96 km (60 mi) west of Warsaw.
