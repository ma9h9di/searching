Weston is a census-designated place[1] located in the southeastern portion of Hazel Dell Township in Pottawattamie County in the state of Iowa. As of the 2010 census the population was 92.[2]

Its location is almost exactly between the cities of Council Bluffs and Underwood near Interstate 80.
