NA-15 (Abbottabad-I) (Urdu: این اے-١٨، ایبٹ آباد-٢‎) is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. The constituency was formerly known as NA-18 (Abbottabad-II) from 1977 to 2018. The name changed to NA-15 (Abbottabad-I) after the delimitation in 2018.
