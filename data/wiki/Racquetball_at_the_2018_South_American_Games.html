Racquetball (Spanish: Ráquetbol), for the 2018 South American Games was held from 27 May to 3 June 2018[1] in Cochabamba, Bolivia.

Tournament format[edit]

The 2018 South American Games racquetball competition has two parts.[2] The individual competitions in Men’s and Women’s Singles and Doubles, and a Team competition. The individual competitions will be held first beginning on May 27 and concluding on June 1. Three of the individual competitions will have a group stage followed by an elimination stage to determine the medalists. The exception is Men’s Doubles, which will be a five country round robin competition. The Team competition will be June 2 and 3. The racquetball venue is Complejo Polifuncional de Sarco.[3]
