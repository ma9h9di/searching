Jordanian military ranks

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Military ranks of Jordan are the military insignia used by the Jordanian Armed Forces. Being a former British protectorate shares a rank structure similar to that of the United Kingdom, but the insignia differs in their visual appearance.

Commissioned officers[edit]

The rank insignia for commissioned officers for the army, navy and air force respectively.
