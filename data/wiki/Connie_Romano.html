Connie Romano (Italian: Connie Romano) is an American actress, producer and host.

Early life[edit]

Concetta (Connie) Romano was born in Newark, New Jersey, US. She is of Italian descent and moved throughout New Jersey before settling in New York. She has studied at HB Studios, New Actors Workshop and before this received a B.S. in business administration from Seton Hall University.[1]

Career[edit]

Connie Romano landed her first gig off her Headshot a short feature "Cute Hearts". She has her own talk show Connie's catch.[2] Which she Hosts and produces. She is in the webseries "Goodstein" [3] playing the lead.
