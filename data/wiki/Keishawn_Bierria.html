Keishawn Bierra (born July 26, 1995) is an American football linebacker for the Denver Broncos of the National Football League. He played college football for Washington. He had five fumble recoveries in 2016, leading the Football Bowl Subdivision. Bierra had 60 tackles (four for loss), two forced fumbles, and two fumble recoveries in 2017.

Professional career[edit]

Bierria was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the sixth round (217th overall) of the 2018 NFL Draft.[1]
