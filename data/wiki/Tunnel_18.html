Tunnel 18 (Persian: تونل 18‎) is a 1997 Iranian Historical drama film written and directed by Hossein Shahabi (Persian: حسین شهابی) [1][2][3][4]

Starring[edit]

  • Ali osivand
  • Reza Naji
  • Bahar Karimzadeh
  • Karim Nobakht
  • Abdolsamad Jodeyri
  • Akram Alamdar
  • Mansoor Sedaghat
