Marko Nikolić (born 17 September 1997 in Huddinge) is a Swedish footballer who most recently played for IF Brommapojkarna in the Allsvenskan as a forward.

Career[edit]

He made his debut in Allsvenskan for AIK 7 July 2014 as a 16-year-old against Åtvidabergs FF.

Nikolic left IF Brommapojkarna at the end of 2018.[1]
