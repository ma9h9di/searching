Alexios Ntanatsidis is a Greek judoka. In 2013, he won the gold medal at the U21 World Championships.[1] In 2014, he won the bronze medal at the European Judo Open Championships.[2] He also won a bronze medal at the 2018 Mediterranean Games.[3]
