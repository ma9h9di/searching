Tommy Engel (2012)

Tommy Engel (German pronunciation: [ˈtɔmiː ˈʔɛŋl̩], Colognian (Kölsch) pronunciation: [ˈtɔmɪ² ˌʔɛŋl̩]) (born 1949) is a German musician and actor. He was the lead singer of German band Bläck Fööss through 1994, and has released albums as a solo performer.[1][2][3]
