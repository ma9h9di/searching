Komorniki [kɔmɔrˈniki] (German: Eichbach) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Polkowice, within Polkowice County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.[1] Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.

It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Polkowice, and 77 kilometres (48 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
