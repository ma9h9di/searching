Events[edit]

January[edit]

  • 11
    • The 17th All Ears festival started in Oslo (January 11–14).[1]
    • The 4th annual Tucson Jazz Festival started in Tucson, Arizona (January 11 – 21).[2]
  • 19 – The 37th annual Djangofestival started on Cosmopolite in Oslo, Norway (January 19 - 20).[3]
  • 28 – The Gustav Lundgren Trio performed a tribute to Django Reinhardt at Stockholm Concert Hall.[4]
  • 31
    • The 7th Bodø Jazz Open started in Bodø, Norway (January 31 – February 3).[5]
    • The 13th Ice Music Festival started in Geilo, Norway (January 31 – February 4).[6]

February[edit]

  • 1 – The 20th Polarjazz Festival started in Longyearbyen, Svalbard (February 1 – 4).[7]
  • 23 – Kirsti Huke received the 2017 Radka Toneff Memorial Award.[8]
  • 25 – The band Hegge was awarded the 2017 jazz Spellemannprisen.[9]

March[edit]

  • 2 – The 14th Jakarta International Java Jazz Festival started in Jakarta, Indonesia (March 2 – 4).[10]
  • 8 – The Turku Jazz Festival started in Åbo (March 8 – 11).[11]
  • 16 – The Blue House Youth Jazz Festival started in Stockholm (March 16 – 18).[12]
  • 23
    • The 19th Cape Town International Jazz Festival started in Cape Town, South Africa (March 23 – 24).[13]
    • The 45th Vossajazz started in Voss, Norway (March 23 – 25). The opening was by Norwegian jazz poet Jan Erik Vold, warning about political maldirections of our time, followed by the opening consert signed Arild Andersen.[14]

April[edit]

  • 23 – The 7th Torino Jazz Festival started in Turin (April 23–30).[15]
  • 25 – The 32nd April Jazz Espoo started (April 25 – 29).[16]
  • 30 – The International Jazz Day.[17]

May[edit]

  • 4
    • The Balejazz started in Balestrand (May 4 – 6).[18]
    • The 29th MaiJazz started in Stavanger, Norway (May 4 – 9).[19]
  • 9 – The 14th AnJazz, the Hamar Jazz Festival started at Hamar, Norway (May 9 – 13).[20]
  • 11 – The 17th All Ears festival started in Oslo (January 11–14).[1]
  • 24 – The 17th Festival Jazz à Saint-Germain-des-Prés started in Paris, France (May 24 – June 4).[21]
  • 25 – The 46th Nattjazz started in Bergen, Norway (May 25 – June 2).[22]

June[edit]

  • 12
    • The Bergenfest started in Bergen (June 12 – 16).[23]
    • The Norwegian Wood music festival started in Oslo (June 12 – 16).[24]

July[edit]

  • 4 – The Kongsberg Jazzfestival opened at Kongsberg consert (July 4 – 7).[25]
  • 6 – The Baltic Jazz festival started in Dalsbruk (July 6 – 8).[26]
  • 7
    • The 40th Copenhagen Jazz Festival start in Copenhagen, Denmark (July 7 – 16).[27]
    • The 22nd Skånevik Bluesfestival started in Skånevik, Norway, with Jeff Beck as headliner (July 7 – 9).[28]
  • 12 – The 17th Stavernfestivalen started in Stavern, Norway (July 12 – 14).[29]
  • 13
    • The 43rd North Sea Jazz Festival started in The Hague, Netherlands ( July 7–9).[30]
    • The 45th Umbria Jazz Festival start in Perugia, Italy (July 13 – 22).[31]
  • 14
    • The 30th Aarhus Jazz Festival started in Aarhus, Denmark (July 14 – 21).[32]
    • The 54th Pori Jazz Festival started in Pori, Finland (July 14 – 22).[33]
  • 16 – The Moldejazz start in Molde (July 16 – 21).[34]
  • 17
    • The 71st Nice Jazz Festival started in Nice, France (July 17 – 21).[35]
    • The 42nd Jazz de Vitoria start in Gasteiz, Spain (July 17 – 21).[36]
  • 25 – The 23rd Canal Street started in Arendal, Norway (July 25 – 28).[37]
  • 27 – The 41st Jazz in Marciac started in Marciac, France (July 27 – August 15).[38]

August[edit]

  • 3
    • The 62nd Newport Jazz Festival started in Newport, Rhode Island (August 3 – 5).[39]
    • The Nišville International Jazz Festival started in Niš, Serbia (August 3 – 13).[40]
  • 8
    • The 19th Øyafestivalen started in Oslo, Norway (August 8 – 11).[41]
    • The 32nd Sildajazz started in Haugesund, Norway (August 8 – 12).[42]
  • 9 – The Tromsø Jazz Festival started in Tromsø (August 9 – 12).[43]
  • 10 – The 34th Brecon Jazz Festival started in Brecon, Wales (August 10 – 12).[44]
  • 11 – The 33rd Oslo Jazzfestival started in Oslo, Norway (August 11 – 18).[45]
  • 17 – The Parkenfestivalen started in Bodø (August 17–18).[46]
  • 30 – The 14th Punktfestivalen opened in Kristiansand (August 30 - September 1|).[47]

September[edit]

  • 21 – The 61st Monterey Jazz Festival started in Monterey, California (September 21 – 23).[48]

October[edit]

  • 17 – The 54th Prague International Jazz Festival started in Prague (March 17 – October 6).[49]
  • 18 – The 36th DølaJazz started in Lillehammer (October 18 – 21).[50]
  • 22 – Vocalist Veronica Swift, the daughter of singer Stephanie Nakasian and the late pianist Hod O’Brien, was attacked on subway platform in New York City.[51]
  • 25 – The 40th Guinness Cork Jazz Festival started in Cork City, Ireland (October 25 – 29).[52]

November[edit]

  • 1 – The 55th JazzFest Berlin, also known as the Berlin Jazz Festival started in Berlin (November 1 – 4).[53]

December[edit]

  • The 24th Umbria Jazz Winter started in Orvieto, Italy (December 20 - January 1).[54]
