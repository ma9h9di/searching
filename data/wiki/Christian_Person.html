Christian Person (born on 26 December 1980) is a German association footballer[1] currently playing for FC Pommern Greifswald as a goalkeeper.

Career[edit]

He previously played for Hertha BSC, 1. FC Magdeburg, FC Carl Zeiss Jena, Dynamo Dresden and Hallescher FC. He had a trial with Scottish Premier League club Hibernian in August 2008.[2]
