York Hamlet is a hamlet and census-designated place in Livingston County, New York, United States. Its population was 544 as of the 2010 census.[1] New York State Route 36 passes through the community.

Geography[edit]

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the community has an area of 2.754 square miles (7.13 km2), all of it land.[1]
