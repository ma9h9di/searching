Ferhadlu (Persian: فرهادلو‎, also Romanized as Ferhādlū)[1] is a village in Mulan Rural District, in the Central District of Kaleybar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 36, in 9 families.[2]
