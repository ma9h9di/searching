Ammi R. Butler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Ammi R. Butler (September 4, 1821 – April 4, 1901) was Mayor of Milwaukee, Wisconsin from 1876 to 1878. He was a Democrat.

He is interred in Forest Home Cemetery in Milwaukee.[1]
